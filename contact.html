BBC Hive is an open source initiative at the BBC



If you are considering using Hive CI in your own deployment and have questions, you can join the https://gitter.im/bbc/hive-ci and ask questions there. We make no guarantees of support but will endevour to help you.

Many parts of the Hive CI platform are now open source and we will be releasing more soon. Follow us on Twitter for more information about this and other parts of the BBC's open source portfolio.
